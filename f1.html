Our society has 4 major houses. (and ambitious for more),
Sports Pundits : Sports enthusiasts sit down to analyze
matches, critique over selection and performance, make
intellectual predictions and observations. Get chances of live
screening commentary and sports quizzes.
Weebs HQ : Anime and Manga fanatics come together to discuss
about their favourite series, discuss writing and depth of stories,
how it affected them and their outlook and letting themselves
express their hearts out.
Series and Chill : similar to Weebs HQ, students would talk on
web series, trying to convince the masses at what makes their
series stand apart and why they should consume it. Praising and
critiquing themes and stories.
Movies and Music : We accept any individual with a passion of
enjoying and expressing music whether they are skilled in it or
not. We aim to provide that to bolster their confidence. They can
express their love for movies here the same way as in series and
chill.
Apart from these 4 Houses, our society also aims to hold
dedicated social events like Movie nights, AnimeCon, Cinema
Hopping, Match screenings, Comic Cons and a Culture Gala.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

The society aims to promote public speaking, communicative skills and critical thinking
by enabling students to express themselves on things they are most passionate about in their
daily digital entertainment. Our society is determined to provide a safe space where folks can express their ideas openly.
That strengthens their ability of self-expression which is a
very important part of practical life.
